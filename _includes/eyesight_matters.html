Eyesight Matters in Health

This course is for you if you work with people in a therapeutic or creative way. It is essential learning for yoga teachers, psychotherapists, massage therapists, Feldenkrais practitioners, art therapists, play therapists, dancers, somatics practitioners and more.

The Learning

  • Exploring how someone's eyesight impacts body, mind and emotion.
  • Learning about common eyesight challenges from a holistic perspective.
  • Understanding simple eyesight prescriptions.
  • What does 20/20 vision really mean? How eyesight is measured.
  • How to integrate eyesight into taking a case study.
  • Why it matters that you know if your client or students wear contact lenses.
