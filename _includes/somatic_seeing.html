Somatic Seeing

Please join me in my explorations teaching embodied vision education. These classes are relaxing and perception enhancing helping you to move into your vision. My aim is that they will feel meditative. The classes will involve some stillness and some gentle movement. Each week we will explore a different visual theme, for example, colour, context, texture, depth, details and more.
